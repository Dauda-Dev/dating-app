Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Ovally ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Ovally mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, date of birth, gender, and profile details you provide during registration and onboarding.
  • Profile content: photos and bio you upload.
  • Location data: approximate location used to show nearby users (only when you grant permission).
  • Usage data: likes, matches, messages, and app activity.
  • Device data: push notification token, device type, and OS version.
  • Payment data: subscription transactions are processed by our payment provider. We do not store full card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with other users based on your preferences and location.
  • To send push notifications about matches and messages.
  • To process subscription payments.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To improve the app experience.

3. Photo Moderation

Profile photos are automatically scanned for inappropriate content using a third-party moderation service (WebPurify). Photos that violate our community standards are rejected and not stored.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: cloud hosting (Render), image storage (Cloudinary), push notifications (Expo), and payment processing (Paystack).
  • Law enforcement: when required by law or to protect safety.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deactivated immediately and permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data (via Settings → Delete Account).
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via device settings.

7. Age Requirement

Ovally is strictly for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we discover an account belongs to a minor, it will be immediately deleted.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication to protect your data.

9. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudinary – image storage
  • Paystack – payment processing
  • Expo – push notifications
  • Daily.co – video calls

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Ovally after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@ovally.app
